The Narrative

Curiosity Driven, Outcome Focused

Sourish Dutta is a commerce student exploring the intersection of finance, technology, and artificial intelligence. Driven by a builder mindset, he doesn't just study markets and systems—he creates solutions that bridge the gap between complex financial concepts and practical digital tools.

With a foundation in rigorous academic training and a relentless curiosity for emerging technologies, Sourish seeks to demystify technical barriers for businesses. His work is characterized by a "calm, intentional precision," aiming for the level of craftsmanship often seen in high-end product ecosystems.
